NBA Salary Distribution

01/12/2021

In this jupyter notebook, I examined salaries in the NBA from 1990 until 2020. In particular, I was interested in measuring how salaries are distributed on teams, how this has changed over time, and how this correlates to win percentage. This jupyter notebook includes all the data scraping and transformation before the exploration of the data and justification of methods used.

For a high level overview, I recommend reading the "Introduction" and jumping ahead to "Spot Checking the Data" where the analysis starts.
